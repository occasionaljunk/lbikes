About Lovely Bikes

LOVELY BIKES is well-known entity inaugurated in the year 2007, at Ludhiana (Punjab, India). Our firm is acknowledged as the leading manufacturer and supplier of bicycle, Ice Cream Carts & customized Trolley. These are manufactured using the best grade raw material that is sourced from some of the most certified vendors of the industry. Our offered products are manufactured by our trained experts at our sophisticated production department. These professionals use advanced technologies and sophisticated
machines to manufacture the offered products in accordance with the worldwide norms of quality. The offered products are
extremely valued by our esteemed patrons for their compact design, durability, corrosion resistance and easy installation. As per the varied requirements of our customers, these products can be customized in variegated options laid by them.

‍

Owing to our advanced infrastructure, we offer superior quality products with in the promised time schedule. Our infrastructure is compartmentalized into different units like production, quality testing, packaging, sales & marketing, etc. We have invested large amount of finance on the construction of our vast infrastructure that sprawl across a large area of land. This unit helps us store our bulk orders in a well-organized manner. Our team of experienced professionals enables us to offer superior quality products. They work in close harmonization with each other to achieve the company’s objective of delivering quality assured products at clients’ end. These professionals help us to provide customization facility and develop the offered range as per the specific requirements of our customers. Owing to their hard work, we have earned a reputed status in this field.

As a quality driven firm, our main motto is to uphold consistent level of premium quality in our offered products. The sourced raw material is tested on various parameters to ensure superiority in our finished products. The Company‘s prime objective is to produce superior quality Products by constant improvements and upgrades recommended by our R&D team. We have experienced quality controllers who conduct rigorous quality checks at different stages of production. Our fair business practices, crystal clear transactions and quality centric approach have made us one of the essential names in the market. We have won great compliment
by fulfilling the varied demands of our patrons in an efficient way. Also, our marketing and sales personnel ensure punctual deliveries of the ordered consignments to our customers.
